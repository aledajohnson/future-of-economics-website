THREE KINDS OF ECONOMIC THINKING

Before comparing frameworks, it helps to understand they're not all answering the same question. These three categories cover different levels of the same conversation.

SYSTEMS ask who owns production and who makes decisions. POLICY SCHOOLS debate how to run an economy within a given system — usually capitalism. ALTERNATIVE FRAMEWORKS step back and ask what the economy should be FOR in the first place. Most public debate happens in the middle layer while the other two go unexamined.
